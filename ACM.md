# HRD Corp Allowable Cost Matrix (ACM) — Developer Reference

> Source: ACM Guidebook (September 2025) + ACM Table (November 2025 Edition)
> Scope: **HCC, SBL and SLB schemes only** | **Grant Submission supporting documents only** (not claim)

---

## 1. HOW TO USE THE MATRIX (Calculator Logic Flow)

1. Determine the **scheme** (HCC / SBL / SLB)
2. Determine the **type of training** (In-house / Public / Overseas / Seminar & Conference / e-Learning / Development Programme)
3. Decide on the **training venue** (Employer's Premise / External Premise or Hotel)
4. Determine **trainer type** (Internal / External / Overseas)
5. Look up **allowable costs** and **supporting documents**

## 2. SCHEME OVERVIEW

### A. HCC — HRD Corp Claimable Courses

| Property | Detail |
|---|---|
| Payment flow | HRD Corp pays training provider directly — employer pays **nothing upfront** |
| Trainer requirement | Must be HRD Corp **certified & accredited** trainers (effective 1 Jan 2025); exceptions: speakers at conferences/seminars |
| Grant submission deadline | At least **1 day before** training starts |
| Upfront payment option | Employer can request up to **30% upfront** during grant application (paid upon grant approval) |
| Eligibility | Must be HRD Corp registered employer, levy paid for first month, **no arrears**, no legal issues |

**Training types available under HCC:**
- In-house training
- Public training
- Remote Online Training / ROT (in-house)
- Remote Online Training / ROT (public)
- Development Programme
- e-Learning
- Coaching and mentoring
- Mobile e-learning

**Grant Submission — Required Supporting Documents (HCC):**
- [ ] Course content with training schedule (including date and time)
- [ ] Accredited trainer profile
- [ ] Invoice **or** quotation for course fees
- [ ] Invoice or quotation for chartered transportation *(if applicable)*
- [ ] HRD Corp Special Approval Letter for Licensed Training Materials (LTM) *(if applicable)*
  - Licensed physical and/or digital training material
  - Any other variations
- [ ] Acknowledgement Letter for Industry Specific or Focus Area courses *(case-by-case)*

---

### B. SBL — Skim Bantuan Latihan

| Property | Detail |
|---|---|
| Payment flow | Employer **pays upfront**, then claims reimbursement from HRD Corp |
| Trainer requirement | Can use **non-registered** training providers and internal trainers |
| Grant submission deadline | Must submit **prior to** start of training |
| Eligibility | Must be HRD Corp registered employer, no legal issues |

**Allowed non-registered trainer/provider types under SBL:**
1. **Internal Trainers** — Employees of the same company or group (incl. overseas subsidiaries) who are subject matter experts; no HRD Corp registration required
2. **Vendors** — Vendors providing services, products or systems that require training
3. **Associations** — Must obtain a one-off approval letter from HRD Corp Training Market Department (TMD) prior to programme
4. **NGOs** — Non-profit voluntary groups (local, national, or international)
5. **Overseas Training Providers** — Based outside Malaysia with no Malaysian entity
   - Local public courses: must collaborate with a locally registered training provider OR get one-off approval from TMD
   - In-house courses: employer must provide justification
6. **Government Bodies** — Organisations/agencies/departments established by the Malaysian government

**Training types available under SBL:**
- In-house training
- Public training
- Remote Online Training / ROT (in-house)
- Remote Online Training / ROT (public)
- Development Programme
- e-Learning
- Coaching and mentoring
- Mobile e-learning

**Grant Submission — Required Supporting Documents (SBL):**
- [ ] Course content with training schedule (including date and time)
- [ ] Trainer profile
- [ ] Invoice **or** quotation of course fees
- [ ] Service or sales agreement between vendor and employer **or** receipt/invoice of item purchase *(if vendor conducts training)*
- [ ] Invoice or quotation for chartered transportation *(if applicable)*
- [ ] HRD Corp Special Approval Letter *(if applicable)*
  - Licensed physical and/or digital training material
  - Any other variations
- [ ] Acknowledgement Letter for Industry Specific or Focus Area courses *(case-by-case)*

---

### C. SLB — Skim Latihan Bersama

| Property | Detail |
|---|---|
| What it is | In-house training shared by **2 or more employers** registered with HRD Corp |
| Organiser role | Appoints/manages trainers, venue, and consumable materials |
| Grant application | Each **participating employer submits their own** grant application |
| Course fee model | Quoted on a **per pax basis**, prorated by final attendance completion |
| Trainer requirement | Only training providers with exemptions (same 5+1 types as SBL) |
| Payment flow | Employer pays upfront, claims reimbursement |

> **SLB is in-house only** (not applicable to public training categories).

**Training types available under SLB:**
- In-house training only (face-to-face or ROT)

**Allowed non-registered trainer/provider types under SLB:**
- Same as SBL: Internal trainers, Vendors, Associations, NGOs, Government bodies, Overseas training providers

**Grant Submission — Required Supporting Documents (SLB):**
- [ ] Course content with training schedule (including date and time)
- [ ] Trainer profile
- [ ] Invoice **or** quotation for course fees *(only ONE invoice/quotation needed per training course)*
- [ ] **Joint Training Letter** from the organising employer containing:
  - Organiser and participants (from employer's side)
  - Name of organiser
  - Course title and training date
  - Training venue
  - Number of pax from each employer
  - Cost breakdown
  - Signature of organising employer
- [ ] Invoice or quotation for chartered transportation *(if applicable)*
- [ ] HRD Corp Special Approval Letter *(if applicable)*
  - Licensed physical and/or digital training material
  - Any other variations

## 3. TRAINING CATEGORIES

These categories apply across schemes and determine the fee caps and rules.

### A. In-House Training
- Customised courses for employees from **one (1) organisation**
- Minimum duration: **4 hours**
- Max trainees: **50/group** (soft skills) | **25/group** (technical)
- Minimum trainees: **2 pax** (face-to-face) | **1 pax** (ROT)
- Applies to: HCC, SBL, SLB

### B. Public Training (Local)
- Courses in Malaysia attended by employees from **more than one company**
- Minimum duration: **4 hours**
- Financial assistance capped at: **max 9 trainees per employer** (exception: actual number considered for public certification programmes)
- Applies to: HCC, SBL *(NOT SLB)*

### C. Public Overseas Training
- Courses conducted **outside Malaysia**
- Minimum duration: **4 hours**
- Max financial assistance: **9 trainees per employer**
- Financial assistance rate: **50%** of total costs
  - **Exception (100% rate):** High technology, new technology, new product development, R&D, engineering, marketing, strategic management — subject to employer justification
- Applies to: HCC, SBL *(NOT SLB)*

### D. Seminars & Conferences
- Seminars: interactive events for discussion and learning
- Conferences: keynote presentations with multiple concurrent sessions
- Minimum duration: **4 hours**
- In-house: min **51 participants/group** (face-to-face or ROT)
- Public: min **1 participant/employer**, min **51 participants/training provider**
- Minimum speakers: **1** (half-day) | **2** (full-day)
- Overseas seminar/conference: **50%** rate of financial assistance

### E. E-Learning
- Customised, self-paced; one organisation
- Minimum duration: **1 hour** with at least **1 complete module**
- No maximum trainees
- ROT (Remote Online Training) is treated the same as face-to-face (in-house or public)
- Rate table:

| Training Hours | e-TRiS Entry | Max Financial Assistance |
|---|---|---|
| 1 hour | 0.1 | RM125/pax |
| 2 hours | 0.2 | RM250/pax |
| 3 hours | 0.3 | RM375/pax |
| 4 hours | 0.5 | RM500/pax |
| 5 hours | 0.7 | RM625/pax |
| 6 hours | 0.8 | RM750/pax |
| 7 hours | 1.0 | RM875/pax |

> For e-learning >7 hours: use additional 0.5 (half-day) or 1.0 (full-day) increments.

### F. Development Programmes
- Diploma, Degree, Masters, PhD, Postgraduate certificate programmes
- Offered by government/private colleges, universities, or TVET institutions (with MSC accreditation)
- Minimum total course duration: **3 months**
- Can be claimed on modular, semester, or whole-duration basis

| Scheme | Additional Requirement |
|---|---|
| HCC | All modules must be **registered with HRD Corp** |
| SBL | Course must be **MQA accredited** (verify at mqa.gov.my/mqr) |

## 4. ALLOWABLE COSTS

### 4.1 Fees

#### Course Fee — General Courses (In-House)

| Duration | Max Rate |
|---|---|
| Full day (≥7 hours) | RM10,500/day/group |
| Half day (4 hours) | RM6,000/half-day/group |

- **<5 trainees:** prorated — `rate ÷ 5 × actual trainees`
  - e.g., 3 trainees: RM10,500 ÷ 5 × 3 = RM6,300
- **SLB proration:** `total group cost ÷ total pax × employer's attending pax`
  - e.g., RM10,500/group, 20 total pax → RM525/pax. Employer A with 7 pax = RM3,675; if 6 attended, approved = RM3,150

#### Course Fee — General Courses (Public/Local)

| Duration | Max Rate |
|---|---|
| Full day (7 hours) | RM1,750/pax/day |
| Half day (4 hours) | RM1,000/pax/half-day |
| Overseas public | As charged, **50% financial assistance** |

#### Focus Area, Industry Specific, Professional Certification Courses
- All: **as charged**
- Quoted on **per pax basis**
- Prorated according to **completion of attendance**
- Focus Areas (9 pillars): Industry 4.0, Green Technology & Renewable Energy, Fintech, Smart Construction, Smart Farming, Aerospace, Blockchain, Micro-credential, Future Technology

#### Internal Trainer Allowance

| Duration | Max Rate |
|---|---|
| Full day (≥7 hours) | RM1,400/day/group |
| Half day (4 hours) | RM800/half-day/group |

- **<5 trainees:** prorated — `rate ÷ 5 × actual trainees`
- Minimum 2 trainees per group for in-house training

### 4.2 Trainee & Trainer Allowance (Travel)

| Distance (one-way) | Max Daily Allowance |
|---|---|
| < 100km | RM250/pax/day |
| ≥ 100km | RM500/pax/day (includes accommodation) |

- Minimum 4 hours for half-day entitlement (claim at half rate)
- **Extra travel day (≥100km):** NOT automatic; employer must apply; only if trainee travels 1 day before or after training; only for full-day (≥7h) programmes; not for half-day programmes
- **No trainee allowance** for trainees based at the organising employer's own premises
- External trainers: allowance covers food, accommodation and land transportation

### 4.3 Meal Allowance

| Duration | Max Rate |
|---|---|
| Full day (≥4 hours) | RM100/pax/day |
| Half day | RM50/pax/day |

> **IMPORTANT:** Employers can claim **either** meal allowance **OR** trainee allowance — **not both**. Subject to training location.

### 4.4 Overseas Trainee Allowance

- Max **RM1,500/pax/day**
- Subject to **50% rate of financial assistance**
- Employers can request up to **2 extra days**

### 4.5 Overseas Trainer Daily Allowance

- Max **RM500/pax/day**
- No supporting document needed

### 4.6 Transportation

#### Chartered Transport
- For in-house programmes conducted **outside** employer's premises (e.g., hotels, external training premises)
- Cost as per quotation from transport provider
- Supporting doc: receipt from transport provider

#### Airfare
- For: trainees/internal trainers from branches; external and overseas trainers
- Actual rate of airfare (includes airport tax, admin fee, fuel surcharge)
- Supporting doc: ticket stub/e-ticket evidence/receipt + invoice from travel agent

### 4.7 Consumable Training Materials

| Amount | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Up to RM100/group | No supporting document needed |
| > RM100/group | Itemised quotation or invoice with price required |

- **SLB:** Only the **organising employer** may claim consumable training materials

### 4.8 Licensed Training Materials (LTM)

- Physical and digital both claimable
- Training provider must obtain **HRD Corp pre-approval** before training
- Employer must include a **copy of HRD Corp approval** in the grant application
- LTM approval validity: **2 years** from approval date

### 4.9 Development Programme Claimable Costs

| Cost Type | Rate |
|---|---|
| Course fees (incl. registration + exam fees) | As per quotation |
| Thesis allowance — Master's (full time) | RM600 |
| Thesis allowance — PhD (full time) | RM1,000 |
| Study allowance — local (full time) | RM900 (prorated if <1 month) |
| Study allowance — overseas (full time) | RM5,000 (prorated if <1 month) |
| Airfare | As per quotation |

**Financial assistance rates for development programmes:**
- Local courses: **100%**
- Overseas courses: **50%**
- Overseas Master's/PhD: course fees **100%**, study allowance & airfare **50%**
- Private higher education institutions (overseas): **100% course fees**

> Employers must fully bear course fees — employees cannot be asked to contribute. Fees paid by employees are not claimable.

## 5. GENERAL RULES & NOTES

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| Minimum attendance | 75% of total training hours; allowance prorated by attendance |
| Half-day threshold | Minimum 4 hours = eligible for half-day amounts |
| Meal vs Trainee allowance | Only ONE type claimable per training — not both |
| SST | Service Tax claimable, as long as total doesn't exceed max claimable amount |
| ROT | Remote Online Training is treated the same as face-to-face equivalent (in-house or public) |
| HRD Corp discretion | HRD Corp may request additional documents; all approvals at HRD Corp's sole discretion |
| Proration trigger | <5 trainees → course fee AND internal trainer allowance are both prorated |
